The first enemy from the upside down

The "Demogorgon" is the first enemy from the Upside-Down dimension seen on the show. It is described as a human-looking creature with no face and long claws like blades in its hands. It is big, fast, and could kill you quickly. However, it has a weakness, which is the hot temperastures, so your better ally against this monster is fire.
